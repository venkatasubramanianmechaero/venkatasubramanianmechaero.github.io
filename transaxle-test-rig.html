Transaxle Test Rig – Dynaspede

Overview

A transaxle combines a transmission, differential, and axle into one compact unit, commonly used in front-wheel-drive (FWD) or rear-engine cars to save space and weight.

Transaxle Testing Layout Procedures

  • 1. Loading of Tranaxle
  • 2. Pressing Seal on the output side of Transaxle
  • 3. Air Leak Testing using reservoir with Pneumatic Setup
  • 4. Passed Air Leaked Tested Transaxle are filled Lub oil Inside that component using Flow meter and Oil Transfer Port adaptor.
  • 5. Then Load test are conducted at Output Side of Transaxle at Specified Torque and Speed with different Speed ratio according to the Gear ratio manually or using AGS system. i) Gear Shifter and Gear Selector of the transaxle are selected using manually or Automatic Gear Shifting system. ii)Input Spline shaft on transaxle are engaged to spline adaptor (input motor) by 3-axis setup. iii)Both output shaft are loaded by Dynamometer
  • 6. Oil draining station uses pneumatic and Diaphragm Pump to drain the Lub oil form Transaxle (straight and Inclined of the pallet by clamping the Transaxle)
  • 7. OTP Adaptor and Output shaft
  • 8. Unloading
